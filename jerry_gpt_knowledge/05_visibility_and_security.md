### The threat (these are the numbers Jerry uses)

| Threat | Data | Source |
|---|---|---|
| On-road truck blind spots | 33% of large-truck/car crashes occur in the "No-Zone" around the truck | FMCSA |
| Pedestrian left-turn risk | 70% higher pedestrian strike risk in left turns for vehicles with large blind zones | IIHS (2025) |
| Construction struck-by | 1 in 4 struck-by deaths involve construction workers. 75% involve heavy equipment | OSHA |
| Forklift severity | 85 forklift fatalities + 34,900 serious injuries per year (US) | OSHA |

**The root cause:** Blind spot incidents are NOT driver-behaviour failures. They are **structural visibility problems** — vehicle geometry, payload obstruction, mixed traffic, low light. This is a problem across four segments: on-road trucking, forklifts/warehouses, construction, agriculture.

### Honest disclosure (visibility)

Cameras cannot see through solid obstructions. Performance depends on installation, lens cleanliness, calibration, target behaviour, weather, dust, vibration, lighting, and changing geometry of loads/booms/attachments.

Position visibility as a **risk-reduction system, not a substitute for safe site design, operator training, mirrors, banksman support, or disciplined operating procedures.** The strongest solutions work inside a broader safety process rather than pretending to replace it.

A poor installation can make a good algorithm look bad. A badly tuned warning can create alarm fatigue. **Credibility comes from solving the real problem — not overselling the technology.**

## SECURITY — The $15B Global Threat

### Market context (use these numbers verbatim)

- **US cargo theft:** $6.6B/year. $18M lost every day. 74% never recovered. Average theft $200K+. (ATRI Oct 2025)
- **US supply chain total impact:** $15–35B annually (Homeland Security)
- **EU cargo theft surge:** €549M reported losses in 2023 — **438% increase in 3 years** (TAPA EMEA)
- **EMEA crime scale:** 157,421 cargo crimes in 129 countries (2022–2024). 76% involve trucks. (TAPA / IUMI / BSI)
- **EU fuel theft:** 70,000 fuel theft cases/year in Germany alone. €100M+ annual EU losses.
- **LatAm violence:** 33% involve armed hijacking. Drivers kidnapped. Brazil = 68% of incidents.
- **Per-carrier cost:** Average carrier $520K/year. Average logistics provider $1.84M/year.
- **Industry response:** ATA added security as strategic priority (2024). Testified 9× before Congress (2025). UK invested £16.5M in secure parking.
- **US strategic theft surge since 2021:** **1,500%**

**The root cause:** Global economic downturn + high inflation. Food & beverage now the #1 stolen commodity (22%). Fuel theft correlates directly with diesel prices. This is no longer a developing-market problem — it's a fully global crisis.

### Honest disclosure (security — internal, don't volunteer)

During hijacking, criminal groups use cellular jammers to block all connectivity. Panic button, real-time alerts, GPS, immobilisation are blocked. The MDVR continues recording locally (evidence preserved). This affects ALL cellular-dependent security systems, not just Streamax. **Credibility requires honesty about what we can and cannot do. Overpromising on security where drivers' lives are at stake is not acceptable.**
