## Next-Gen AI Dashcam — DC MAX (+ GT1 gateway)

**"Pushing the boundary of the AI dashcam."** Merging video with telematics.

- **DC MAX** — flagship AI dashcam. **Dual 2K ultra-high-resolution lenses** (2K
  road-facing + 2K driver-facing), powerful onboard AI processing, compact form
  factor. Supports up to **6 channels of video expansion** (AHD + AI IPC over
  Ethernet — e.g. C46 / C29N / C53 / C40W external AI cameras). Internal AI does
  ADAS + DSC. **Launching Q3/Q4 2025.**
- **GT1** — independent telematics gateway pairing with DC MAX; highly customizable;
  the foundation of the connected-fleet stack. Integrates I/O, GPS, CAN, G-Sensor,
  LTE, Bluetooth, WiFi.
- **Onboard large AI model:** **2.4× the AI compute** of the previous flagship
  (AD Plus 2.0) and **10× efficiency** in new-AI-feature development (large model in
  early development; release TBD).
- **Dual storage — capacity meets certainty:** eMMC up to **256 GB** + MicroSD up to
  **1 TB**. Optional sync makes eMMC the fallback — recover footage even if the SD is
  lost. **Tamper-proof by design:** read-only eMMC seals critical footage —
  protected from theft, tampering, and heat.
- **Wider vision, clearer night;** privacy cover option available.

**Solution: Maximum six-channel video telematics** — DC MAX (dual-lens 4MP+4MP,
internal ADAS/DSC, eMMC) + up to 6 expansion channels (IPC1–4 AHD, plus AI IPC over
Ethernet), optional display, alarm I/O.

## Cargo Security & Trailer Solution — "Extending security into the mobile world"

**The problem:** fuel + cargo theft causes estimated **$17–35 billion/year** in
losses. Existing solutions fall short: sensor-based = high power consumption +
difficult install + no evidence; video-based = difficult install.

**Two value pillars:** Transportation Efficiency (reduce empty miles, reduce idle
days, intelligent order-trailer matching by real-time location + loading rate) and
Cargo Security (record cargo damage, prevent cargo theft).

**In-cargo cameras — "See it all, prove it all":** dual **4K + 1080P blacklight**
cameras for ultra clarity even in the darkest trailer.

**AI cargo-volume detection — "wasted space, wasted money":** AI-powered volume
detection adapts to any cargo / any trailer at **95% average accuracy**. Plus
automatic **overtime / anomaly alerts** ("lost time, lost control") to keep loading
on schedule.

**All-in-one cargo-security device** (launching **Q4 2025**): low power makes
continuous smart detection + video recording feasible with easy deployment.
Interfaces: 4G, WiFi/BT, GNSS, RS-232, I/O, storage; magnetic installation bracket.
**Continuous video recording + suspicious-activity detection for 90 DAYS post
ignition.** **Blacklight Ultra** guards the truck day and night (0.02-lux low-light
comparison).
