## Loading Operations

**Excavator bucket-tooth detachment detection.** A detached/fractured/worn bucket
tooth that reaches the crusher can cause equipment failure costing **up to $1M**.
The system does real-time detection of detachment, fracture, and wear → real-time
voice alert → reported to platform (responsibility defined post-event). Expandable
to large-particle detection.

**Large block detection.** Frequent large-block obstruction at the discharge outlet
causes downtime (**~$50,000/hour**) and, if it causes injury/overturning, **>$300,000
per incident**. The Large Block Detection System uses an evidence-stacking algorithm,
real-time voice alerts, and evidence upload to platform.

**Thermal Smart CAM** — thermal imaging for loading-area hazards.

## Transportation Safety

**Pain points of mining-vehicle operation:** (1) driver risk operations — fatigue,
distraction, speeding, illegal/unauthorized departure; (2) low-light conditions —
following too closely, night, fog → collisions; (3) huge blind areas on mining
trucks — poor visibility → collisions; (4) poor visibility during dumping — no video
assistance, side/rear collision risk.

**System components (schematic):** M10 Mining MDVR (core recorder) + Smart DMS +
communication system; front-collision warning; 270° blind-spot warning; rear-view
radar-visual fusion; driver-cabin camera; reverse rear-view camera; front blind-spot
camera; display; beeper; radar.

### Edge-Computing Driver Monitoring (Mining DMS IPC)
Edge-computing DMS purpose-tuned for mines: **fatigue detection that works through a
gas mask** (no false alarm for mask), distinguishes two-way radio vs. mobile phone
use. Detects **both hands leaving the steering wheel** and **unauthorized departure
from the seat**. (Stats from 5 mines, non-public: avg 4 safety incidents/mine/year
2021–2023 from improper gear shifting, hands off wheel, and unauthorized departure.)

### Forward Collision Warning (Mining ADAS IPC)
Windshield-mounted smart device with built-in computing + AI deep learning for
accurate distance-to-vehicle-ahead. Industry-unique collision-warning solution built
for mining: algorithms for **no-lane scenes, mine-road meetings, and alarm
suppression** on different road sections; precise distance measurement, graded
warnings, suited to low-speed bumpy environments.

### Blacklight Smart Camera
Identifies pedestrians + vehicles in **extreme dark (0.01–0.03 lux)** — built for
night mine operations. Precise distance measurement within 100m; accurate trend
warnings to minimize false alarms / driver distraction.

### Radar-Vision Fusion (for dusty scenes)
Integrates **millimeter-wave radar** (penetrates dust) with camera vision — solves
scenarios where radar OR camera alone fails. Accurate alarms under extreme
(dusty/low-light) conditions.

### V2X / V2V (beyond visual range)
Vehicle-to-vehicle communication **independent of network infrastructure**, not
limited by line-of-sight. Features: collision-risk warning, emergency warning,
comprehensive traffic perception, vehicle-location awareness.

### Blind Spot Detection (Mining BSD IPC)
The **first intelligent blind-spot warning system for mining**, a **BSD dual-lens
blacklight camera**. Identifies pedestrians AND smaller vehicles (pickups, SUVs,
sedans, vans) simultaneously — critical because pedestrians and pickups are too
small to see from a mining truck. Trained on extensive mining-specific algorithms.
Blacklight enables warnings in extremely low light. **Widest warning range in the
industry — 270° dead-zone-free.** Side detection ~41m forward range / 12.42m,
compatible with wide-body and mining trucks. Validated at a China Gold mine and a
copper mine.

### Reverse Radar-Vision Fusion + Intelligent Reversing Camera
Misjudging distance when backing up causes overturns — loss minor (vehicle damage /
injury >$1M) to major (fatalities, business suspension). **High-performance reverse
radar:** centimeter-level distance measurement, waterproof, dustproof,
low-temperature resistant. **World's first intelligent reversing camera:** before,
the driver couldn't see the distance between tires and the wall; after, they can —
plus intelligent detection of large rocks — helping meet safety requirements and
reducing tire damage from rocks during reversing.

## Intelligent Management Platform — MineSync-Cloud + SafeGPT

**MineSync-Cloud** — next-gen production + transportation safety platform powered by
big data and large-scale video AI (Web · APP · Open API). **Video Engine:** 99.9%
reliability, millisecond preview, cross-platform.

**SafeGPT** (world-class industry safety large model) — four engines:
- **Safety Assistant** — detect millions of risks, pinpoint the key one; targeted training for highest-concern drivers.
- **O&M Assistant** — full device health monitoring, customized management reports + data output.
- **Agent Assistant** — improves safety-manager efficiency; automatic event handling + reporting.
- **Efficiency Optimization Engine** — learn from experienced drivers, set standards, real-time guidance for standardized operation.

**Dispatch / operation efficiency** solves four problems: route & task guidance,
abnormal alerts + dispatch, dispatch optimization (reduce empty runs / waiting), and
production monitoring. Plus refined cost accounting (trip statistics, energy
consumption, material cost), data-driven optimization (route/equipment/management),
and **AI detection for fixed scenarios** (intrusion detection, conveyor-belt safety
inspection, compliance detection).
