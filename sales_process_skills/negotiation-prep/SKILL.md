# Negotiation Prep — Streamax

Principle: **if you're not ready to walk, you're not ready to sell.** A full
pipeline is your leverage.

## Prepare a Give/Get list
Columns: what you'll GIVE, what you'll GET, and the VALUE of each. Rank by
importance. Give reluctantly, slowly, and NEVER without getting ("the only way I
can do something for you is if you do something for me — like combining Phase I
and II this quarter, or a multi-year commit").

## Make stands on P / V / V / C
Plan for ~4 squeezes; make ~4 stands on **Pain, Vision, Value, Control** (not
price). Follow each stand with a conditional offer (a get for a give).

## Smart-buyer tactics to expect (don't panic)
Never sole-source; never tell you you're winning/losing; **negotiate in reverse
preference order** (grind underdogs, then use it on you); **take it away at least
once** ("budget got cut, we're going elsewhere"); exploit your quarter-end. Counter
with quantified value, knowing your column, and willingness to walk.

## Phase III = risk, not price
Late-stage stalls/price fights are usually RISK fear. Sell risk reduction —
local teams in 8 countries, proven 15-min installs, reference fleets, SafeGPT
track record — instead of cutting price (a big discount can even INCREASE their
risk fear).
