# Business Development Prompter (pain-based cold outreach) — Streamax

Goal: create CURIOSITY ("tell me more"), NOT a meeting or a sale. It does not
pitch product.

## Structure
1. Your name (just the facts; "you and I haven't spoken before").
2. Your company + credibility (world #1 video telematics, 5M vehicles, 100+
   countries) — no product dump.
3. Industry expertise ("we've worked with fleets/TSPs for X years").
4. THEIR job title — show you know their world.
5. A **menu of pains** for that specific title (a Safety Director's pains != a
   CFO's != a TSP owner's).
6. Invitation: "Would you be interested in how we helped [peer] solve this?"

## Tailor the pain menu to the title
- Safety Director: fatigue events before they become accidents; 500 nuisance
  alerts/day; can't prove not-at-fault.
- CFO: insurance premiums; false-claim payouts; paying for a tracker AND a camera.
- VP Fleet/Ops: downtime, disputed claims, driver churn.
- TSP owner: Samsara/Motive poaching your tracking accounts direct; $0 video
  revenue; no way to defend the account.

## Reference Story (pair it)
Short, third-party, same title: "A VP Fleet at a 400-truck carrier was missing
safety targets — with SafeGPT they cut at-fault accidents ~30% and premiums ~15%
in a year." Ends with a measurable result, then the invitation.

Reserve **sacred prospecting time** — latent pain is where Column A is created.
