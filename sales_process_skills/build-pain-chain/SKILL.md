# Pain Chain — Streamax

A picture where each level's pain is the REASON for the level above it. Confirm it
with the buyer; it differentiates you and shows where the money is.

## Trucking-fleet example
- Drivers / Safety Manager: harsh events, fatigue incidents, nuisance-alert
  fatigue -> safety KPIs missed.
- Ops / VP Fleet: at-fault accidents, downtime, disputed claims -> uptime & cost
  targets missed.
- CFO: insurance premiums up, false-claim payouts, self-insured retention hits ->
  margin pressure.
- CEO: safety record threatens contracts, brand, valuation -> growth at risk.

## How to use it
1. Start from the person you're with, name their pain + the reasons.
2. Ask "who above you feels this, and how?" — link upward until you hit power.
3. Confirm the whole chain back to the buyer.
4. Size the deal across the chain (SafeGPT for safety, trailer cams for cargo/CFO,
   compliance cameras for regulatory) and pick who to sell to.

School bus, transit, and mining have their own chains (child-safety/liability;
passenger incidents/regulator; haul-road collisions/production loss) — adapt the
levels to the vertical.
